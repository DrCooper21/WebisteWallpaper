Our Story

Since there are only a few 8K wallpapers in the big, wide internet that slow down global warming, we founded this website. . .

The prinzip

After you downloaded a wallpaper, a 20s unscipable add will occure. From the owned Money we donate 10% to a sustainable project, wich plant for every Wallpaper a tree. So far we planted around 10 000 trees, but with your help we plant more.
